About me

So how did I end up here after working in childcare, retail and participated in volunteering? Well... I left my job because I wanted a change of career but I wasn't too sure what to do next. Then, I came across a tweet about learning valuable skills and coding was one of them. I did some research and found a few websites however, none of them appealed to me (they looked gloomy and boring). Nevertheless, I discovered SheCodes, which offered a free 60-minute coding session. Next thing you know, I was part of the SheCodes community, learning to code! Coding has taught me skills such as being patient, identifying bugs and rectifying errors, using my initiative and being prepared when learning new materials. I have also been taught how to use Bootstrap, JavaScript, HTML and CSS. It is an opportunity to express myself through my work whilst learning, and I believe that these skills will help me succeed in my future career as a developer. Overall, I love to learn, after all knowledge it power.
