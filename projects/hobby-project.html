Dog walking🐕🚶🏽

A hobby that keeps me fit and my dog happy

Walking has many benefits for my health as well as my dog's; since Smoki has a lot of energy, she needs plenty of exercise in order to keep in shape! Exploring new places keeps our walk interesting and Smoki intrigued. It is also crucial to stay hydrated during our walks, as it keeps our levels of concentration up!

There are many advantages of dog walking such as:

  • ❤️Lowers blood pressure
  • 🧡decreases stress levels
  • 💛Improves mental health
  • 💚Encourages socialisation
  • 💙Reduces obesity
  • 💜Builds stronger muscles, joints and bones
